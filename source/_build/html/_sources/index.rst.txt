Introduction
============

.. _Fireanote: https://www.fireanote.com

Fireanote_ is a simple notification system for your apps. Integration is a snap 
with a single call to a URI. Receive notifications in your email inbox.

.. image:: _static/images/welcome.png
   :width: 400
   :alt: Welcome to fireanote screen shot

All notifications are destroyed and nothing is stored.

That's all it is:- a simple to use notification system.

Having said that there are some bells-n-whistles if you so wish, such as increased
capacity, or sentiment analysis.
